Self Drive Cars on Rent in Goa

Rent self-drive cars in Goa for your next trip. Best suited for your travel needs when you want to discover everything yourself and need to save time. Best suited for friends, families, couples, and business travel. Rent a car and take it anywhere within Goa. Simple per day rates for an unbounded renting experience.

Unlimited Kilometers

All of our cars have unlimited kilometers, so there’s no limit on how much you travel within Goa. You only need to take care of the fuel. It’s advised to fill up your tank at a nearby station, as in some village areas of Goa, finding one without Google Maps can be challenging if you’re new here.

Proper Permits

We have proper legal permits for all our cars to drive within Goa. All our vehicles are approved for rental by the state transport department and are allowed in both North and South Goa.

Minimum Paperwork Required

We require minimal paperwork for renting. You only need a valid photo ID and a valid driving license with the legal driving age.

Per Day Rates

One day of rental is counted from 9 AM to 9 AM the next day, with pricing based on that. For pricing queries, call us, and we’ll offer the best per-day rates. Discounts are available for extended rentals—contact us for the best offer!
